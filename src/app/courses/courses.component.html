FreeCodeCamp

freeCodeCamp is a non-profit organization that provides a comprehensive curriculum of coding challenges and projects, as well as a platform for learners to practice and build their skills. It was created in 2014 with the goal of helping people learn to code for free, and it has since grown to become one of the most popular coding education platforms on the web. On freeCodeCamp, you can learn a wide range of coding languages and technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, React, and more. The platform is organized into a series of tracks that guide learners through the curriculum and help them build real-world projects, such as web applications and data visualization tools. freeCodeCamp is completely free to use and does not require any payment or subscriptions. It is supported by a community of volunteers and donors who contribute their time and resources to help make coding education accessible to everyone.
